About

It`s a web-service, based on Prozorro. This service gives you an ability to search for tenders by their hash. Tenders have 3 status: Unsuccessful (Either tender hasn`t started yet, or it was cancelled), active and complete. If you search for some tenders, they will be automatically added to your search history. You may check your history in the profile. If status of some of the tenders you have in your history changes, you get email about that.
